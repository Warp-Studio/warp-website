Built for India,
by India.

We're a small, focused studio obsessed with building tools that make life simpler for Indian merchants navigating the Shopify ecosystem.

Our Mission

Great software for the world's most ambitious merchants.

India's e-commerce market is unlike anywhere else in the world: complex tax rules, unique compliance requirements, and a diverse merchant base that ranges from first-time entrepreneurs to large distributors. Yet most Shopify apps are built for a Western audience, leaving Indian merchants to patch things together themselves.

"We build the apps we wish had existed when we first opened our Shopify store."

Warp Studio was born out of that frustration. We believe Indian merchants deserve software that actually understands their reality, GST, GSTIN, HSN codes, inter-state taxes, and all. Not as an afterthought, but as the entire point.

What we stand for

Our principles.

01
Precision over features
We'd rather do one thing exceptionally well than ship ten mediocre features. Every detail in our apps is deliberate, from the invoice layout to the error messages.
02
India-first thinking
We don't bolt on India support as an option. We design every feature from the ground up for how Indian commerce actually works, the regulations, the workflows, the expectations.
03
Radical simplicity
Our merchants are busy running businesses, not learning software. If something takes more than one click, we haven't designed it well enough yet.
Why India

The opportunity hiding in plain sight.

India has over 150,000 Shopify merchants and the number is growing fast. But GST compliance, introduced in 2017 remains one of the most common pain points for store owners. Manual invoicing, spreadsheet reconciliation, and CA fees eat into margins that are already thin.

We saw a clear gap: no one was building a truly great GST invoicing experience for Shopify India. So we built it ourselves. And we're just getting started.
